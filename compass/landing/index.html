The problem

Agent fleets scale faster than your visibility into them.

01

Your agents are running. You just don't know how well.

Logs tell you an agent fired. They don't tell you whether it finished the job, how much it cost, or whether last week's version did it better.

02

Token spend ≠ output quality.

Most teams can't tell the difference. An agent reading an 80k-token document to produce a 10-line summary looks "busy" — it's actually your most expensive mistake.

03

One bad prompt change and your best agent regresses.

Quality drops the day someone "improves" the prompt. Without a baseline, you find out weeks later — from a customer.

How it works

Observe → Evaluate → Recommend → Act.

The agent health loop. Connect once, then every token your fleet spends gets justified — or flagged.

STEP 1

Connect your agents

Log files, an API wrapper, or a thin SDK. Compass captures inputs, outputs, latency, token cost, and completion signals.

STEP 2

Compass scores each agent

Cost efficiency, output quality, task completion rate, prompt efficiency — one health score per agent, every day.

STEP 3

Recommendations surface

"Agent 4 is reading 40k tokens when it needs 400." Specific, ranked by dollars saved — not generic dashboards.

STEP 4

One click to act

Fix the prompt, clone the winner, or add a guardrail. Then watch the score move.
